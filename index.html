Live Deployment · Makurdi, Benue State

Offline-First Hotel Management System

The Challenge

A Makurdi hotel was suffering from frequent internet connectivity drops causing operational delays — resulting in failed check-ins, lost booking records, and frustrated staff unable to access the management system during peak hours.

Our Solution

We engineered a highly optimized, multi-tenant Hotel Management System built on an Offline-First architecture. Core operations — check-in, check-out, room assignment, billing — continue working seamlessly with zero internet. Data syncs automatically and securely the moment connectivity is restored.
